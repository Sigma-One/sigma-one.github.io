Overview

The nuclear mod is a mod designed to improve Factorio's nuclear power production mechanics, which I've always found too simple. the mod adds plutonium, MOX-fuel and other things to accomplish this.

Features

  • Plutonium as a fission product
  • MOX-fuel cells, made from plutonium and U-238
  • Uranium ore must go through a hexafluoride phase before being enriched
  • Fluorine ore must be mined for hexafluoride
